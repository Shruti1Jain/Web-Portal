ELECTRICAL AND ELECTRONIC ENGINEERING




Vision

To impact knowledge of Electrical and Electronic Engineering and prepare graduates to achieve excellence in engineering education and research.

Mission
                
  1. To prepare students with deep understanfinf of fundamentals of electrical an electronics engineering.
  2. To prepare professionals with positive attitude, values and vision.
  3. To collaborate with industry, research organisations and academic to encourage innovation
  4. To provide a platform for engineering grauduates to create and design new products and system that can help industry and society as a whole.
