INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY





Vision

To produce information technology engineers who work passionately, creatively and effectively for the betterment of technology and society at large.

Mission
                
  1. The missoin of Information Technology branch is to educate students in technology and provide advanced knowledge in related areas in order to enable them to create and consume information products for a dynamic information society
  2. The aim is to create a culture that fosters excellence and combines rigorous academic study with the support of a diverse campus community.
  3. The endeavour is to have up-to-date curricula and pedagogy in the information technology discipline so that students have a solid foundation in the core concepts and develop problem solving skills.
  4. The mission is to offer internship opportunities to the students and to foster their personal and professional growth. .
